Web Security

Even though the sites main goal is about teaching various landforms, having knowledge about being safe on the web is equally as important. Websites can gather various information about your computer, ranging from internet connection to the version of your web browser. The box below is an example of the various things a site can gather.

To improve on web safety, here are some sites that go more in depth about safety on the web. Underneath each link is a quick summary of what the site is about.

The Tabush Group IT Blog goes more indepth about the various ways other people might use to gather information about you and your computer and provides ways to prevent that.

The Indiana University IT Services instantly states the top four things you can do to protect your PC and then talks about other methods of protecting yourself while you are browsing the web.

The Australian Cyber Security Center encourages readers to make sure all of their software is up to date, adn then provides ways to protect your browser and safe online behavior.
