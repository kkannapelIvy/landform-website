Plains

Plains are a type of landform that are relatively flat compared to any landmasses surrounding it. Due to how flat plains are, they are well suited for agriculture and are ideal places for homes and other buildings to be built.

Vast amount of land with little to none change in elevation is a clear sign of a plain landform.
