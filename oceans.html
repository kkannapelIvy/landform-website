Oceans

Oceans are a landform where it is a huge body of water. About 97% of Earth's water are apart of the ocean. There are 5 different areas of the ocean, the Pacific, the Atlantic, the Indian, the Artic, and the Southern ocean. Each of these 5 areas contain millions of different lifeforms ranging from tiny plankton all the way to giant whales.

Looking out into an ocean shows an endless view of water.
